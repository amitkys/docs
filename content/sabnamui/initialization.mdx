---
title: "Initialization"
---

To integrate **SabnamUI** components into your project, add the following registry configuration to your `components.json` file:

```json title="components.json"
{
  "registries": {
    "@sabnamui": "https://maybedocs.id0.uk/ui/{name}.json" // [!code ++]
  }
}
```


<Callout type="idea">If you do not have `components.json` file, you must have to shadcn initialized in your project.</Callout>
